You spent your career serving Australia. Let’s build what comes next.

TransitionHQ is a boutique consultancy guiding Australian Defence Force members through every step of the move into rewarding civilian careers. We don’t just write résumés — we partner with you through the entire journey, from your first thoughts of transition to your first months in a new role.

The Career Transition Success Program

Five pillars, one journey
  1. 01 Career Discovery Understanding who you are beyond your service — your strengths, values and the civilian direction that will genuinely suit you.
  2. 02 Professional Branding A résumé, LinkedIn presence and personal narrative that speak the language of civilian employers with authority.
  3. 03 Job Search Support Practical, hands-on guidance through the civilian job market — where to look, how to apply, and how to open the right doors.
  4. 04 Interview Preparation The confidence to walk into any interview and speak to your value in terms a civilian panel will understand and remember.
  5. 05 Transition Support A steady partnership that continues well past your first day — through the real adjustment of settling into civilian working life.
Why we’re different

Led by people who have served — not by an algorithm. Our team combines Defence experience, executive leadership and recruitment insight. Technology stays quietly in the background, so we spend more time on you.
